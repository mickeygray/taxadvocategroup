Tax Overview: Nevada

Nevada does not levy a state individual income tax. State tax issues are more commonly tied to business taxes including sales/use tax, the Commerce Tax on businesses with Nevada gross revenue exceeding $4 million, and the Modified Business Tax on wages. Individual residents primarily face federal tax obligations.

Key Tax Facts

  • No state individual income tax
  • State tax issues primarily business-related (sales/use, Commerce Tax, Modified Business Tax)
  • Federal tax obligations apply fully — IRS maintains collection authority
  • 6.85% state sales tax plus local additions — among the highest combined rates when including local
